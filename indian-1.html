Harmonium
The harmonium is a keyboard instrument in which notes are produced by air driven through metal reeds by foot-operated bellows. It is a modified version of the reed organ.
Embroidery
Embroidery is the method used for decorating fabrics with a needle and a thread. The decorative stitching technique allows for realistic and abstract designs to be stitched on a fabric.
